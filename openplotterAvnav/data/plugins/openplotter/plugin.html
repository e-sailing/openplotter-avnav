* this page is not based on a designed interface and will probably not work in the future!
It enables quick presetting of the settings parameters.
All settings made can be set reliably via the settings page. It concerns:
- Settings->Widgets->Widget Base Font(px)->14 | 12 | 10
- Settings->Widgets->2 widget rows->uncheck
- Settings->Buttons->Button Size(px)->50 | 42 | 35
- Settings->Buttons->2 button columns->checked
- Settings->Layout->Layout name->system.plugin.system-openplotter.default | system.defaults
- Settings->Layout->Base Font(px)->14 | 12 | 10
- Settings->Map->float map behind buttons->checked
